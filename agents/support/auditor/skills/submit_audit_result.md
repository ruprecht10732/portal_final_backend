# Skill: SubmitAuditResult

## Purpose

Persist the final audit verdict and explicit findings for a visit report or call log.

## Use When

- Submitted evidence has been compared against intake expectations.

## Required Inputs

- Pass or fail result.
- Findings and missing evidence.

## Outputs

- Durable audit result record.

## Side Effects

- Informs human review and operational follow-up.

## Failure Policy

- A failed audit should clearly state what evidence is missing.
