# Skill: GetMyJobs

## Purpose

List the accepted jobs that belong to the current partner.

## Use When

- The partner asks what jobs they have today, this week, or in general.
- The conversation needs a first narrowing step before selecting one specific job.

## Output Policy

- Summarize jobs briefly with the most useful distinguishing details, such as city, appointment time, or service type.
- Keep the list concise and easy to scan in WhatsApp.

## Failure Policy

- If no accepted jobs are found, state that plainly.
- If one job clearly matches the current conversation, continue directly with that job.
