# Skill: UpdateAppointmentStatus

## Purpose

Update the status of the resolved partner appointment.

## Use When

- The partner wants to mark a visit as completed, cancelled, requested, scheduled, or no-show.

## Workflow

1. Resolve the exact appointment first.
2. Normalize the requested status to one supported by the tool.
3. Confirm the successful update in one short Dutch reply.

## Failure Policy

- If the appointment is not yet resolved, ask one short clarifying question.
- If the requested status is unsupported or unclear, ask the partner to restate it more concretely.
