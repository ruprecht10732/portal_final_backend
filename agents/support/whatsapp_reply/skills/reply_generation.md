# Skill: ReplyGeneration

## Purpose

Draft one grounded WhatsApp reply suggestion for a human operator.

## Use When

- Conversation assistance is requested for a WhatsApp thread.

## Required Inputs

- Lead, quote, appointment, timeline, and recent conversation context when available.

## Outputs

- One tenant-tone-aligned WhatsApp reply draft.

## Side Effects

- None beyond suggested text.

## Failure Policy

- Keep the draft concise, natural, and operationally correct.
- Never promise facts, pricing, or appointments that are not supported by the record.
