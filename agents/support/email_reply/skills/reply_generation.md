# Skill: ReplyGeneration

## Purpose

Draft one grounded email reply suggestion for a human operator.

## Use When

- Communication assistance is requested for an inbound email thread.

## Required Inputs

- Lead, service, quote, appointment, timeline, and inbound email context when available.

## Outputs

- One tenant-tone-aligned email reply draft.

## Side Effects

- None beyond suggested text.

## Failure Policy

- Keep the draft useful, professional, and grounded.
- Never fabricate state, promises, or scheduling outcomes.
