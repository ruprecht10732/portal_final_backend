# Skill: AskCustomerClarification

## Purpose

Draft a concise clarification request for the customer when intake is still too incomplete for safe progression.

## Use When

- The best next action is to ask the customer instead of guessing.

## Required Inputs

- The minimum missing information needed to unblock the next step.

## Outputs

- A clarification draft suitable for downstream communication handling.

## Side Effects

- No direct database write by itself; it shapes customer follow-up.

## Failure Policy

- Ask only the most critical unresolved items.
- Keep the message in Dutch and operationally useful.
