# Skill: CalculateEstimate

## Purpose

Persist the estimate derived from trusted scope, quantities, labor, and material assumptions.

## Use When

- Quantities and inputs are known well enough for bounded pricing.

## Required Inputs

- Labor and material components.
- Explicit quantities, units, and assumptions.

## Outputs

- Durable estimate state for the service.

## Side Effects

- Feeds quote drafting and later fulfillment decisions.

## Failure Policy

- Do not use mental math for totals.
- If material uncertainty still affects price safety, stop and surface the blocker.
