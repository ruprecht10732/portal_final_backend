# Skill: ListCatalogGaps

## Purpose

Record missing or unsafe catalog coverage when no reliable product match exists.

## Use When

- An autonomous estimation pass reveals recurring or blocking catalog blind spots.

## Required Inputs

- The missing item or unresolved product need.
- Why the catalog is insufficient.

## Outputs

- Structured catalog-gap record.

## Side Effects

- Makes pricing uncertainty explicit for humans and later analysis.

## Failure Policy

- Use this instead of silently substituting an unverified product.
