# Skill: SubmitQuoteCritique

## Purpose

Persist whether a draft quote is acceptable or requires repair.

## Use When

- A quote review loop has identified concrete defects or confirmed readiness.

## Required Inputs

- Verdict and specific issues.
- Actionable repair guidance when needed.

## Outputs

- Durable quote critique record.

## Side Effects

- Feeds quote-repair loops and human review.

## Failure Policy

- Critique must stay specific and actionable.
- Do not use vague feedback that cannot guide repair.
